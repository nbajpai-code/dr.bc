## 🎯 Key Concepts

* **Business Continuity (BC):** The proactive planning and preparation to ensure that an organization can continue to operate its critical business functions during and after a disruption.
* **Disaster Recovery (DR):** A subset of BC focused specifically on recovering IT infrastructure, systems, and data after a disruption.
* **RTO (Recovery Time Objective):** The maximum tolerable length of time that a computer, system, network, or application can be down after a failure or disaster occurs.
* **RPO (Recovery Point Objective):** The maximum targeted period in which data (transactions) might be lost from an IT service due to a major incident.
* **BIA (Business Impact Analysis):** The systematic process to determine and evaluate the potential effects of an interruption to critical business operations.
